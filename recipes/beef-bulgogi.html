Beef Bulgogi Bowl

A simple, quick, easy and healthy meal for lunches or dinners for the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb Ribeye
  • 1 Inch Knob Ginger, Finely Chopped
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1/2 Cup Canned Pears, Drained and Rinsed
  • 1/3 Soy Sauce
  • 3 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Toasted Sesame Oil
  • 1 Tbsp Chile Garlic Sauce
  • 2 Cups Premade Kimchi
  • 1 Large Cucumber
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

  1. Partially freeze ribeye, then cut in thin slices
  2. Blend the pears, ginger, garlic, soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil and chile garlic together
  3. Marinade steak strips in glass dish for 2 hours
  4. Heat oil in cast iron pan on high, sear steak strips to preferred temperature
  5. Slice cucumber
  6. Lay steak strips, cucumber, kimchi and any preferred veggies on bed of rice
  7. Enjoy!!!
