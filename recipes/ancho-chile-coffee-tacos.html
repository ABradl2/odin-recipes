Ancho Chile Coffee Rub Tacos

This recipe is sure to WOW any of your friends.

They are quick, easy and delicious.

Ingredients

Ancho Coffee Skirt Steak
  • 14 Oz Skirt Steak
  • 2 Tbsp Finely Ground Coffee
  • 2 Tbsp Ancho Chile Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Kosher Salt
  • 1 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • Zest of 1 Lime

Creamy Corn Salsa
  • 2 Ears of Corn
  • 1 Cup Mexican Crema
  • 1 Jalapeno, seeded and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 Cup Cilantro, Finely Chopped
  • 1/2 Medium White Onion, Diced
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • 1 Tbsp Ancho Coffee Rub from Previous Step
  • 12 Corn Tortillas

  1. Mix all the seasonings together and rub the skirt steak with the mix, saving 1 tbsp
  2. In a cast iron pan, heat 2 Tbsp of oil or butter on high for 5 minutes
  3. Sear seasoned skirt steak to personal preference
  4. Mix all Salsa Ingredients together, cook in cast iron pan until done
