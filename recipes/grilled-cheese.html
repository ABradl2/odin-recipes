Grilled Cheese

A timeless classic for any day, or night, of the week.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Slice of Gouda
  • 1 Slice of Swiss
  • 4 Strips of Freshly Cooked Bacon
  • Parsley for Garnish
  • 1 Tbsp of Butter
  • 2 Slices of Bread of Your Choosing

  1. Place butter in pan on medium heat
  2. Soak 2 pieces of bread in melted butter on both side
  3. Place cheese on top of both slices of bread, reduce heat to low
  4. Allow cheese to fully melt
  5. Combine both slices of bread
  6. Increase heat to medium, cook until golden brown
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley
